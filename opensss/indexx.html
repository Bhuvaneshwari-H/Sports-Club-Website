The Sport Club Program is comprised of sport clubs formed, developed, governed, and administered by the student membership working in conjunction with the Office of Intramurals and Recreation, a unit of the Division of Student Life. The key to the success of this program is student leadership, interest, involvement, and participation. The Sport Club Council with advisement of the Office of Intramurals and Recreation has created this manual in order to provide officers with the structural framework necessary for clubs to gain recognition, develop leadership skills, and run safe, efficient programs.
